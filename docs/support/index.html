Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Forged, tap the Settings tab, then tap the premium card and choose "Restore Purchase." Make sure you're signed in to the Apple ID you used to originally purchase Forged Premium.

I purchased Forged Premium but the app still shows the free tier. What do I do?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchase." If the issue persists, force-quit the app and relaunch it. If you're still having trouble, contact us at the email above.

Is Forged Premium a subscription?

No. Forged Premium is a one-time purchase of $3.99. You pay once and unlock all features forever — no recurring charges, no annual renewals.

How do I delete a habit?

Long-press any habit card on the Today screen to open the edit screen, then scroll down and tap "Delete Habit."

Can I back up or export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings and tap "Export Data" to download a JSON backup of all your habits and completion history. You can also import a backup from the same screen.

How do streaks work?

A streak counts the number of consecutive days you've completed a habit according to its schedule. Daily habits require a check-off every day. Weekly habits (e.g. 3x per week) require you to hit your target each week. Missing a scheduled day resets your streak.

How do I request a refund?

Purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with the Apple ID you used to purchase.
